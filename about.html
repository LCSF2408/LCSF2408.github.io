Upright Vacuum Cleaner

A powerful, high-capacity vacuum designed for deep carpet cleaning and large areas. With advanced brush roll technology, it picks up dirt and pet hair effortlessly.

Benefits

  • Ideal for large carpeted areas and pet owners.
  • Convenient, no-bag design for easy emptying and maintenance.
  • HEPA filter traps allergens, making it suitable for households with asthma or allergies.

Handheld Vacuum Cleaner

A compact and lightweight vacuum for small cleanups and hard-to-reach areas. Perfect for quick spot cleaning of furniture, car interiors, and stairs.

Benefits

  • Cordless operation for quick and hassle-free cleanups.
  • Ideal for cars, furniture, and small areas in the home.
  • Lightweight and portable, making it easy to carry and use in tight spaces.

Robotic Vacuum Cleaner

An intelligent, self-operating vacuum cleaner that navigates and cleans autonomously. Equipped with mapping technology for efficient cleaning and returns to the charging dock automatically.

Benefits

  • Set-and-forget convenience with automatic scheduling.
  • Multi-mode cleaning adapts to different floor types and needs
  • Great for maintaining floors between deep cleanings with minimal effort.

Stick Vacuum Cleaner

A lightweight, versatile vacuum cleaner ideal for everyday use on floors and rugs. Transforms into a handheld vacuum for extra flexibility, perfect for quick and easy cleanups.

Benefits

  • Cordless design with a long battery life for uninterrupted cleaning.
  • 2-in-1 function as both stick and handheld vacuum for versatile use.
  • Lightweight and easy to maneuver, perfect for apartments and small spaces.
